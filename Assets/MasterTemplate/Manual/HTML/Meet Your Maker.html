Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Meet Your Maker

On the Subject of Meet Your Maker

But will you meet YOUR maker?

See Appendix M for module creator identification reference.
  • When this needy module activates, an image of a solvable modded Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes module will appear on the left-hand side of the module.
  • Select the name of one of the creators* of the displayed module within 40 seconds to deactivate the module. If no name is chosen within 40 seconds, a strike will be issued.
  • Selecting a module creator name who did not have a part in creating the shown module will cause a strike and deactivate the module.

If the module fails to connect to the repository, a yellow warning triangle will appear and the module will use a hardcoded list of modules shown in Appendix M.

*Anybody who is credited as a Developer or Manual Author on the Repository of Manual Pages is listed as a creator of a module.
